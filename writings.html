Serenity in Chaos

In the heart of chaos, I found my peace,
Where silence whispers, and all worries cease.
Among the stars, my thoughts take flight,
Embracing darkness, becoming light.

"في هدوء الفوضى وجدت نفسي"

Waves of Thought

Like the ocean, my mind ebbs and flows,
With every tide, a new idea grows.
Endless horizons, dreams set free,
In every wave, a part of me.
